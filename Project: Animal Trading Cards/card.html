Black Mamba

Unlike most mambas, the black mamba prefers terrestial habitats, rather than trees.

  • Scientific Name: Dendroaspis polylepis
  • Average length: 2.5m
  • Average Lifespan: 8 years
  • Habitat: Forests

The black mamba is a highly venomous snake found in sub-Saharan Africa. It is usually grey in colour and has an inky-black mouth. Its elegant yet deadly, agressive and delivers a series of bites in rapid successions.
